# FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Air Boss.

## General

### What is Air Boss?
Air Boss is a flight club management app that helps aviation clubs manage aircraft, members, scheduling, and flight operations.

### Is Air Boss free?

### What platforms does Air Boss support?
- iOS (iPhone and iPad)
- Android phones and tablets
- Web browsers (any modern browser)

## Account & Profile

### How do I reset my password?
1. On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Create a new password

### Can I change my email address?
Contact your club admin or support to change your account email.

### How do I delete my account?
Contact support to request account deletion.

## Clubs & Membership

### How do I join a club?
Search for your club by name and request to join. Your club admin will approve your membership.

### Can I be in multiple clubs?
Yes! You can join multiple clubs and switch between them in the app.

### How long does membership approval take?
It depends on your club admin. Most approvals happen within 24-48 hours.

## Documents

### What documents do I need to upload?
Each club sets their own requirements. Common documents include:
- Pilot Certificate
- Medical Certificate
- BFR (Biennial Flight Review)
- Government ID

### Why aren't my documents verified yet?
Document verification is done manually by club admins. Contact your admin if it's been more than a few days.

### Can I update my own document expiration dates?
Yes, you can update expiration dates on your own documents. Admins can also update them for you.

## Scheduling

### How far in advance can I book?
This depends on your club's settings. Check with your club admin.

### Can I cancel a reservation?
Yes, you can cancel your own reservations anytime before the flight.

### What if someone else has the plane I want?
You'll need to wait for an open slot or contact the other pilot to coordinate.

## Flights

### How do I log a flight?
After completing your flight, open your reservation and tap **Complete Flight**. Enter your ending Hobbs/Tach times and any notes.

### Can I edit a flight log after submitting?
Contact your club admin to make changes to submitted flight logs.

## Squawks

### What is a squawk?
A squawk is a report of an aircraft issue or discrepancy that needs attention.

### Should I fly with an open squawk?
It depends on the squawk severity. Check with your club admin or maintenance personnel before flying with open squawks.

## Technical Issues

### The app isn't loading / is slow
Try:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close and reopen the app
3. Update to the latest version
4. Contact support if issues persist

### I found a bug!
Please report bugs through the in-app feedback widget or contact support. Include:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Screenshots if possible
