# Mobile App Overview

The Air Boss mobile app is available for iOS and Android devices.

## Download

- **iOS:** [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/air-boss-flight-fleet-ops/id6472218943)
- **Android:** [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mycompany.airboss)

## Key Features

### Home Dashboard

Your personalized dashboard shows:
- Active flights in progress
- Upcoming reservations
- Recent club activity
- Quick actions
- Club weather snapshot

### Aircraft

- View your club's fleet
- See aircraft details, photos, and specs
- Check current Hobbs/Tach readings
- View maintenance status and squawks

### Schedule

- Calendar view of all reservations
- Book aircraft for your flights
- See who has aircraft reserved
- Avoid scheduling conflicts

### Flights

- Log completed flights
- Record Hobbs and Tach times
- Track flight hours
- View your flight history
- Support maintenance flight workflows

### Squawks

- Report aircraft issues
- Track squawk status (open, in progress, resolved)
- Add photos and descriptions
- View squawk history
