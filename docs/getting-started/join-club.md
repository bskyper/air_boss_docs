# Joining a Club

Learn how to join an existing flight club on Air Boss.

## Finding Your Club

If your club already uses Air Boss:

1. Ask your club admin for the **club key** and **club password**
2. Open the Air Boss app
3. Tap **Join Club** on the home screen
4. Enter your **club key**
5. Enter your **club password**

## Requesting Membership

1. Submit your club credentials
2. Tap **Request to Join**
3. Your request will be sent to the club admin for approval

:::note
Some clubs require admin approval before you can access club features. You'll receive a notification when your request is approved.
:::

If your account is still pending, you may see a dedicated **Pending Approval** screen until an admin activates your membership.

## After Approval

Once approved, you'll be able to:

- View the club's aircraft fleet
- See the flight schedule
- Book available aircraft
- Log your flights
- Report squawks

## Multiple Clubs

You can be a member of multiple clubs. Use each club's key/password to join, then use the club switcher to move between them.
